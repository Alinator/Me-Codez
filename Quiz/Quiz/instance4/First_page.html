Welcome to the gamification quiz!


This has been developed by two students from Stockholm university at the
department of Computer and System Sciences. This work is related to a bachelor thesis.
The aim of the project is to highlight and examine the gamification influence on people's minds and
as well as see what type of gamification elements that are most effective in changing people's motivation
for education.

For you safety, no personal data except your name will be stored in our database, and this is because of analysis reasons.
Otherwise no personal data is required or will be asked from you.

The test works like this that you will be presented by some questions. There are three levels in this quiz and for each
rightly answered question you will recieve points and achievements. There is also a leaderboard in which you can see your
position as a contestant compared to other contestants.
Please do try to complete all of the three levels so we can benefit from your experience that you will gained by using our
test tool.

We thank you for your cooperation and good luck with the quiz!


Ali Nazar & Michel Nickbon
Stockholm University
